ZION Youth Development Initiative

Individual Project Site Plan - W05 Assignment

Site Name

ZION Youth Development Initiative

This name reflects an organization focused on empowering young people through education, mentorship, and community development. It conveys strength, growth, and purpose. The name aligns with the organization’s mission to raise a new generation of leaders.

Optional domain: zionyouths.org

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an online hub for youth empowerment, providing details about programs, training opportunities, mentorship, and community projects. It will also help visitors learn about ZION’s mission, register for events, and connect through volunteer and partnership forms.

Scenarios

  • How can I register for youth empowerment programs or skill development workshops?
  • Where can I find information on volunteering or partnering with the organization?
  • What community outreach activities are currently running, and how can I join?

Color Schema

The chosen colors represent growth, energy, and positivity — values that align with youth development.

  • Forest Green (#1f6b4f): Used for headings, buttons, and accents (symbolizing growth and hope).
  • Sunset Orange (#ff8a3d): Used for links, highlights, and call-to-action elements (representing energy and inspiration).
  • Paper White (#f7f9fb): Used for the background to ensure clean and readable content.

Typography

The typography reinforces professionalism and readability.

  • Playfair Display: Used for headings to give a bold, elegant look.
  • Roboto: Used for body text, providing clarity and modern readability.
