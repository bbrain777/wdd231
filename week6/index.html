What We Do

From homework help to coding bootcamps, ZYDI provides safe spaces for young people to build confidence, skills, and community.

Values: Inclusion • Learning • Service • Faith • Resilience

Why It Matters

Families face real barriers—cost, access, and time. We lower those barriers with community-led programs that deliver measurable outcomes.

Impact: rising grades, new tech skills, and stronger support networks.
