About ZYDI

We empower youth through education, technology, leadership, and service—building a purpose-driven generation.

Leadership Team

Olakunle Tayo Obademi — President & Founder

Olakunle Tayo Obademi is the President and Founder of the Zion Youth Development Initiative (ZYDI), a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering young people through education, technology, leadership, and community service. With a strong background in software development and youth mentorship, he combines his passion for innovation and social impact to create sustainable programs that inspire growth and transformation among young people.

Driven by a vision to build a generation of purpose-driven leaders, Olakunle promotes skill development, creativity, and moral excellence among youths. He continues to advocate for education, digital empowerment, and community development as tools for national progress.

Mrs Tolulope Obademi — Co-Founder

Tolulope Obademi is the Co-Founder of the Zion Youth Development Initiative (ZYDI) and a dedicated Registered Nurse at North Tees Hospital in the United Kingdom. Passionate about compassion-driven service and community well-being, she brings her professional healthcare experience into the organization’s mission of empowering and nurturing young people.

Her commitment to youth development, health awareness, and humanitarian service inspires many of ZYDI’s initiatives aimed at improving the lives of children and families. Tolulope believes that building a healthy and purpose-driven generation begins with care, education, and positive mentorship.

Akomolafe Adedayo — Director of Events

Akomolafe coordinates ZYDI’s outreach calendars and impact days, ensuring our programs are effective, safe, and community-centered.

Temitope Obademi — Publicity Director

Temitope leads public communications, content, and event promotions to tell authentic stories of growth and service.
